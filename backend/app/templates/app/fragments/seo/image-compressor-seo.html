Compress Images Without Guesswork

Reduce file size while preserving visual quality. Preview results, compare before/after, and export in modern formats for faster pages and smaller emails.

Guidelines

  • Choose formats wisely: WebP/AVIF for photos, PNG/SVG for flat graphics.
  • Balance quality vs size: Adjust compression until artifacts are acceptable.
  • Resize large photos: Downscaling often saves more than compression alone.

Workflows

  • Prepare assets for blogs, landing pages, and newsletters.
  • Optimize screenshots for tickets and documentation.
