Track Word and Character Counts as You Write

See words, characters (with and without spaces), and sentence counts in real time. It’s handy for essays, abstracts, social posts, and anywhere limits matter.

Helpful Uses

  • Stay within submission limits for forms or academic work.
  • Balance post length for readability and engagement.
  • Estimate reading time alongside counts when planning content.

Suggestions

  • Sentence detection is heuristic—proofread for nuance and flow.
  • Consider headings and lists separately when optimizing structure.
