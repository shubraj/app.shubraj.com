Decode Cloudflare‑Protected Email Addresses

Cloudflare obfuscates emails in HTML to reduce scraping. Paste the encoded snippet to reveal the original address so you can contact site owners or audit your own pages.

When It’s Useful

  • Customer support: Recover an address when the page hides it behind JavaScript.
  • QA and audits: Confirm that your site’s obfuscation works and decodes correctly.
  • Migrations: Extract addresses before moving content between platforms.

Notes

  • Only decode content you’re authorized to use; respect site terms.
  • Obfuscation is not encryption—treat decoded emails as personal data.
