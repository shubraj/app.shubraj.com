Resize Images to Exact Dimensions

Set width and height, preserve aspect ratio, and export in common formats. Ideal for thumbnails, social banners, and email‑friendly versions of large photos.

Tips

  • Resize before compressing to avoid double work.
  • Keep originals; resizing is a one‑way operation.
  • Use vector formats when you need crisp scaling without blur.
