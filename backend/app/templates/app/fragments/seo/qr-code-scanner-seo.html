Scan QR Codes Directly in Your Browser

Use your device camera to scan QR codes without installing an app. Useful for quick checks, testing generated codes, or accessing links on a desktop.

Make Scanning Easier

  • Ensure adequate lighting and hold the code steady.
  • Fill most of the camera view to help the decoder.
  • Clean the lens if autofocus struggles.

Privacy

Scanning happens locally in your browser. Camera access is only used to detect and decode the QR image in view.
