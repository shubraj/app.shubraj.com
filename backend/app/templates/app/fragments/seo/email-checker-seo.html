Check Email Syntax and Common Pitfalls Before Sending

Quickly validate address formatting and catch issues that cause bounces. This tool focuses on pragmatic checks—structure, allowed characters, and obvious typos in domains.

What It Catches

  • Syntax errors: Missing @, misplaced dots, invalid characters.
  • Suspicious domains: Frequent misspellings of popular providers.
  • Edge cases: Quoted local parts or long TLDs handled conservatively.

What It Doesn’t Do

  • No SMTP verification. Use your ESP or a backend service for inbox existence checks.
  • No deliverability scoring—it’s a fast pre‑flight, not a full audit.

Tips

  • Use double opt‑in to confirm ownership.
  • Keep a suppression list to avoid repeat bounces.
