Make IPv4 Subnetting Less Error‑Prone

Calculate network, broadcast, host range, and usable hosts from a CIDR or IP/mask quickly. It’s a lightweight helper for network planning, lab setups, and exam practice.

What You Can Check

  • Network and broadcast addresses for a given CIDR.
  • Wildcard mask and dotted‑decimal netmask equivalents.
  • First and last usable host addresses and total host count.

Tips

  • Remember that /31 and /32 have special semantics for point‑to‑point and hosts.
  • Document subnets alongside VLANs to avoid overlap and confusion.
