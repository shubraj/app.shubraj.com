Create QR Codes That Scan Reliably

Generate QR codes for URLs, text, Wi‑Fi credentials, or contact details. Controls for error correction and size help ensure good scans on screens and printed materials.

Tips for Better Scans

  • Use sufficient contrast—dark code on a light background.
  • Avoid placing codes on busy images; leave quiet zones around the edges.
  • Pick higher error correction for small prints or potential damage.

Usage Ideas

  • Link to product pages, menus, and event schedules.
  • Share Wi‑Fi settings without typing credentials.
  • Embed contact information for quick saves.
