Convert Unix Timestamps and Human‑Readable Dates

Translate between epoch seconds/milliseconds and formatted dates for logs, APIs, and databases. Quick conversions help when correlating events or debugging time math.

Common Tasks

  • Convert timestamps from logs to readable dates.
  • Generate epoch values for API requests and fixtures.
  • Toggle UTC vs local to match how your app displays time.

Tips

  • Check whether values are in seconds or milliseconds—mix‑ups are common.
  • Normalize to UTC in storage; convert to local time at the UI layer.
