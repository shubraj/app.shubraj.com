Pick Exact Colors from Any Image

Upload an image and sample pixels to get HEX, RGB, and HSL values. Lock swatches, copy code‑ready values, and build palettes for UI themes or brand assets.

Practical Tips

  • Zoom in to sample edges or anti‑aliased text precisely.
  • Average samples over a small area to reduce noise.
  • Record palette notes so teammates know where colors came from.

Use Cases

  • Match brand colors from screenshots or PDFs.
  • Extract UI colors from design mockups.
