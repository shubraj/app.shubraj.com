What the DV Photo Tool Checks

Our DV Photo Tool evaluates your photo based on the official requirements, including:

  • Color: The photo must be in color, not black and white.
  • Size: 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) with a resolution of 600x600 pixels.
  • File Format: JPEG (.jpg) format only.
  • File Size: No more than 240 kB.
  • Aspect Ratio: A square aspect ratio is required.
  • Recency: Taken within the last six months.
  • Background: Plain white or off-white background.
  • Head Position: Centered, occupying 50-70% of the photo.
  • Eye Position: Eyes positioned 1 to 1 3/8 inches from the bottom.
  • Lighting: Even lighting with no shadows.
  • No Accessories: No glasses or items covering the face.

Tips for Taking a Great DV Lottery Program Photo

Follow these tips to improve your chances of capturing a compliant photo for the DV Lottery:

  • Use a plain, light-colored wall as a background.
  • Ensure balanced lighting to avoid shadows.
  • Avoid wearing hats, glasses, or other accessories unless required for religious reasons.
  • Keep a neutral expression, looking directly at the camera.
